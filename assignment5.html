About Maharashtra food

Maharashtra cuisine includes mild and spicy dishes.Wheat, rice, jowar, bajri, vegetables, lentils and fruits are dietary staples. Peanuts and cashews are often served with vegetables. Meat was traditionally used sparesely or only by the well off util recently, because of economic conditions and culture.
